## Crafting Guidelines

### Time & Requirements

- Crafting time = **[X days per bonus trait]**
- **Minimum of 1 day**, even for 0-trait items
- Use [CORE SYSTEM NAME] for base item stats unless replaced
- Must possess relevant **Ability level** for the item crafted
- Modified items require **exclusive crafting time** during the downtime period

### Craft Levels & Modification Access

- **Crafts 3**: One **standard modification**
- **Crafts 4**: One **special modification chop** or another standard
- **Crafts 5**: One **special chop** and may stack or reattempt for multiple standard options

## Modification System

### Standard Modifications

Choose from:

- Gain a **Bonus Trait**
- Remove a **Negative Trait**
- Deliver or soak **+1 damage**
- Adjust **rate of use/fire**

> Each mod may only be chosen **once per item**, unless otherwise allowed.

### Special Modification Chop

**Chop** = a system of randomization, e.g., Rock-Paper-Scissors or equivalent.

#### On a Win

- Receive a **positive feature** based on item type  
  - *Example*: “Silent Shot” (gun), “Serrated Edge” (blade)

#### On a Tie

- Choose **one standard mod** as described above

#### On a Loss

- Gain a **negative mod** assigned by ST  
  - *Example*: “Breaks Easily”, “Loud”, “Heavy Drawback”

> Negative effects may stack. A sword could be “Fragile x2”.

### Defensive
Armor provides its own Health Levels that you mark off its Item Card first when you take damage. When all its Health Levels have been marked off, the armor is too damaged to provide any further benefit. Some armors offer only protection against damage from certain sources, such as firearms or melee weapons.

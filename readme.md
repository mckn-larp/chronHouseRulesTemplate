## Signing In

By signing into this game, all participants affirm their understanding and acceptance of the chronicle’s house rules. This includes:

- Adherence to the chronicle’s specific rulings listed below.
- Compliance with [Parent Ruleset] and the broader policies of [Organization Name].
- Acknowledgement of storyteller authority to interpret or adjudicate any ambiguous situation in real time.

### Proxies

Our chronicle **[allows / does not allow]** proxy characters.

- If proxies are allowed:
  - Proxies are considered **[soft/hard] proxies**.
  - They are subject to the same in-character consequences as any other character.
  - Signing in as a proxy constitutes implied consent to participation and potential risks, including narrative consequences.
- If proxies are disallowed:
  - All players must be present to portray their own characters.
  - Exceptions must be pre-approved by staff.

# [CHRONICLE NAME]: Wraith the Oblivion Character Creation Template (Oblivion MET)

---

This template provides a flexible framework for building **Wraith characters** using the *Oblivion* LARP system (*Oblivion*) and the OWBN Guides. It includes guidelines for selecting key traits, Arcanoi, and other supernatural elements tied to the Shadowlands.

---

## Character Creation Steps

1. **Choose a Legion**  
   Select the **Legion** your character belongs to. This reflects how your character died and shapes their afterlife duties.
   - Examples: **Emerald Legion (War dead)**, **Iron Legion (Criminals)**, **Silent Legion (Unknown dead)**, etc.

2. **Choose Nature & Demeanor**  
   These determine how your character regains **Willpower** and present themselves socially.

3. **Assign Attributes**  
   Distribute **7/5/3** dots across Physical, Social, and Mental categories.
   - No more than **5 in any one category** at creation.
   - Traits reflect your character’s power in challenges.

4. **Assign Abilities**  
   Distribute **13 dots** among Talents, Skills, and Knowledges.
   - No single Ability may begin above **3 dots** without a solid justification.

5. **Choose Backgrounds**  
   Spend up to **5 dots** on Wraith-specific Backgrounds:
   - Examples include **Artifact**, **Haunt**, **Memoriam**, **Mentor**, **Status**, **Contacts**
   - All backgrounds must be **justified in the backstory**.
   - [Chronicle may define starting limits or restrictions.]

6. **Choose Arcanoi (Powers)**  
   Choose **3 dots** worth of Arcanoi.
   - These are the supernatural powers of Wraiths (e.g., Argos, Keening, Moliate, Puppetry).
   - All Arcanoi must be **approved** and **match your Legion, Guild, or history**.

7. **Assign Passions & Fetters**  
   - Choose **3 Passions** (major emotional drivers from life). Assign a total of **5 dots** across them.
   - Choose **5 Fetters** (anchors to the living world). Assign a total of **7 dots** across them.
   - Passions and Fetters are sources of **Pathos** (your energy pool) and **Powers**.
   - [STs may approve alternate allocations.]

8. **Choose Starting Corpus**  
   All Wraiths begin with **10 Corpus levels** unless modified by traits or powers.
   - Corpus acts as your health pool and fuel for some powers.

9. **Assign Shadow Traits**  
   Define your **Shadow**, the dark reflection of your Wraith.
   - Choose a **Dark Archetype** (e.g., Monster, Director, Tempter)
   - Choose **Thorns** (dark powers), usually **1 Thorn** at start (STs may allow 2)
   - Name your **Shadowguide** (a player or ST role) or accept a random assignment.

10. **Assign Freebie Points**  
   Spend **15 Freebie Points** as follows:
   - Attribute: 5 points per dot  
   - Ability: 2 points per dot  
   - Background: 1 point per dot  
   - Arcanos: 5 points per dot  
   - Willpower: 1 point per dot  
   - Pathos: 2 points per dot  
   - Thorns: 7 points per Thorn  
   - Passions/Fetters: 1 point per extra dot

11. **Spend Starting XP (Optional)**  
   [Chronicle may offer 30 XP at character approval.]  
   XP may be spent per standard *Laws of the Underworld* rates.

12. **Submit Backstory & Approvals**  
   Your sheet is not fully legal until your **background is approved**.  
   - Required for Background validation and any special approval elements (custom Fetters, R&U Arcanoi, etc.)
   - Backgrounds should explain **Passions, Fetters, Shadow origins**, and **how you died**.

---

## Chronicle Customizations

You may optionally define:

- **Allowed Guilds**: STs may allow or restrict Arcanos Guild membership at creation.
- **Customized Pathos Recovery**: Whether Memoriam or Fetters are more available in the local setting.
- **Haunt Rules**: Whether Haunts function as shared zones or exclusive territory.
- **Hierarchy, Renegades, Heretics**: Chronicle-specific factions players may affiliate with.

---

## Rare & Unusual (R&U) Requests

STs must approve characters with the following:
[ Examples only]
- **Doppelgangers / Eidolons / Dual-Templates**
- **Risen**
- **Wraiths with non-standard causes of death**
- **High-powered Shadows or multiple Thorns**
- **Characters with high Corpus or unorthodox Arcanos loadouts**

- **Nature/Demeanor**: Survivor / Rebel

  - “Destroy the corrupt” (1)

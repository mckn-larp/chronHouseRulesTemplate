# [CHRONICLE NAME]: Demon the Fallen Character Creation Template

---

This template outlines the standard character creation process for **Demon: The Fallen** in a [Mind’s Eye Theatre / OWBN / custom] chronicle. It is designed to be modular and adaptable to local storyteller rulings, factional themes, or specific chronicle needs. Use it to create Earthbound, Redeemers, Raveners, and more.

## Character Creation Steps

1. **Choose a House (Fallen Caste)**  
   - Select from the **Five Houses** of the Fallen:  
     - **Devils (Namaru)** – Leaders, tempters, and lords of the First House.  
     - **Scourges (Asharu)** – Messengers, healers, and angels of mercy.  
     - **Malefactors (Annunaki)** – Builders and earthbinders, now corrupted craftsmen.  
     - **Fiends (Neberu)** – Seers, dream-walkers, and mind-benders.  
     - **Defilers (Lammasu)** – Inspirers, artists, and seducers of humanity.

2. **Choose a Faction**  
   - Your fallen aligns with one of the political groupings of Hell’s return:
     - **Raveners**, **Reconcilers**, **Cryptics**, **Luciferans**, **Faustians**, or **Earthbound**.  
   - Chronicle staff may require approval for Earthbound or extreme factional builds.

3. **Select a Host Form**  
   - Choose the type of mortal your demon inhabits:  
     - Dead (recent corpse), dying (comatose or mortally wounded), or living (possessed with permission).  
   - This affects Willpower, Humanity, and potential Advantages/Disadvantages.

4. **Choose Nature and Demeanor**  
   - Determines how your character regains **Willpower** and reflects their psychological mask.

5. **Assign Attributes (Physical, Social, Mental)**  
   - Distribute **7/5/3** across the three categories.  
   - Points may be spent beyond this with freebies or XP.  
   - [Set your chronicle's trait cap.]

6. **Assign Abilities**  
   - Distribute **11 Ability points** across Talents, Skills, and Knowledges.  
   - No Ability should start higher than 3 without approval.  
   - [List any locally required Abilities, e.g., Lore or Theology.]

7. **Choose Lores**  
   - Select **3 Basic Lore paths**, each from the following:  
     - One from **House-specific** Lore  
     - One from **Common** Lore  
     - One from any appropriate path  
   - You start with **3 dots in one** and **1 dot in each of the other two**.  
   - Only **Basic-level** Lores are allowed at creation.

8. **Assign Faith, Torment, and Willpower**  
   - **Faith**: Starts at 3.  
   - **Torment**: Starts at 3 (adjusted by character history and choices).  
   - **Willpower**: Based on your host and role; typically starts at 4–5.

9. **Assign Backgrounds**  
   - You may take up to **5 different Backgrounds**, up to **3 dots each**.  
   - [Specify if certain backgrounds are banned, like Cults, or require approval.]

10. **Select Merits and Flaws**  
   - Up to **7 points** in Flaws allowed at creation (grant equal Freebie Points).  
   - Merits must be pre-approved by STs.  
   - [List any banned Merits/Flaws.]

11. **Spend Freebie Points**  
   - Begin with **15 Freebie Points**.
   - Costs:  
     - Attribute: 5 / dot  
     - Ability: 2 / dot  
     - Lore: 7 / dot  
     - Faith: 7 / dot  
     - Willpower: 1 / dot  
     - Torment: 1 / dot (to reduce)  
     - Background: 1 / dot  
     - Merits: 1 per point value  

12. **Receive Starting XP**  
   - Characters receive **30 XP** unless otherwise specified by staff.  
   - XP may be spent during creation or banked for post-approval advancement.

13. **Submit Background and Sheet**  
   - Finalized characters must include:
     - Complete MET character sheet
     - 1–2 page background explaining:
       - Their Fall
       - The host they inhabit
       - Their goals and faction motivations
   - Required within **[X] days** of first play.

## Sample: Fiend (Neberu) Reconciling Visionary

- House: Neberu (Fiends)  
- Faction: Reconcilers  
- Host: Suicidal Psychic (dying, saved at last moment)  
- Nature: Visionary / Demeanor: Penitent  
- Attributes: Mental 7, Social 5, Physical 3  
- Abilities: Awareness 2, Occult 3, Investigation 3, Empathy 2, Subterfuge 1  
- Lores: Lore of Humanity 3, Lore of Portals 1, Lore of Radiance 1  
- Faith: 3 / Willpower: 5 / Torment: 3  
- Backgrounds: Cult 2, Mentor 2, Resources 1  
- Merits: Medium (2), Enchanting Voice (2)  
- Flaws: Haunted (2), Enemy: Former Exorcist (3)  
- Freebies: Used on Willpower, Lores, Abilities  
- XP: 30 XP used to increase Lore of Humanity to 4 and gain Occult 4  
- Notes: Seeks to reweave torn mortal minds and guide other Fallen away from Ravener corruption

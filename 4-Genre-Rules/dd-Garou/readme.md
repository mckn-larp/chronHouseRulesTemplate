## Character Creation Steps

1. **Choose Breed, Auspice, and Tribe (or Breed/Tribe/Species for Fera)**  
   - [Insert list of allowed Breeds, Auspices, Tribes, or Fera groups.]
   - These determine access to Gifts, Renown preferences, social dynamics, and supernatural outlook.

2. **Choose a Nature and Demeanor**  
   - [Outline expectations or local restrictions.]

3. **Choose Starting Rank**  
   - **Rank 1 (Cliath):** Free
   - **Rank 2+:** [Specify cost, background needs, and approval requirements.]

4. **Assign Traits**  
   - Starting: 7/5/3 (Physical / Social / Mental)
   - Additional traits cost 1 point each.
   - Max of 5 per category without ST approval.

5. **Assign Abilities**  
   - Cost: 1 point per dot.
   - No Ability should exceed 3 dots at creation without strong concept justification.

6. **Assign Tempers (Rage, Gnosis, Willpower)**  
   - Free base: Rage 1 / Gnosis 2 / Willpower 1
   - Modified by Breed, Auspice, Tribe.
   - Additional dots cost 3 points each.
   - May not exceed standard caps per book.

7. **Choose Basic Gifts**  
   - One Gift each from your Breed, Auspice, and Tribe is recommended.
   - Cost: 3 points per Gift.
   - Only Basic-level Gifts are permitted at creation.

8. **Assign Backgrounds**  
   - Up to 14 dots at creation.
   - Cost: 1 point per dot.
   - Must be supported by character background.

9. **Apply Breed, Auspice, and Tribe Bonuses**  
   - [Insert any local modifications or clarifications.]

10. **Assign Renown**  
   - Rank 1: 3 Temporary Renown (mixed)
   - Rank 2+: Permanent Renown must be purchased (2 points per dot)
   - Additional Temporary Renown subject to ST approval.

11. **Select Flaws & Negative Traits**  
   - Up to 7 points of Flaws (gain equal bonus points).
   - Up to 5 Negative Traits (gain 1 point per Trait).
   - Must suit character concept.

12. **Bonus Points & Starting XP**  
   - Freebie Points: 5
   - Starting XP: 30 (use standard XP costs)

13. **Finalize Sheet and Submit**  
   - Sheet and background must be submitted within [X days] of approval.
   - Background must support Traits and Backgrounds taken.

---

## Advancement Notes

- Characters use a **160-point build pool** (excluding XP and freebies).
- Point costs:
  - Traits & Abilities: 1 per dot
  - Tempers: 3 per dot
  - Gifts: 3 per Basic Gift
- No Ability or Trait may exceed 5 at creation without ST approval.

## Fera Characters

- Fera follow the same system unless otherwise noted.
- Use appropriate Changing Breeds book for templates and mechanics.
- Rank 2+ requires background + ST approval.
- Renown cost for higher Rank: 2 XP per dot of Permanent Renown required.

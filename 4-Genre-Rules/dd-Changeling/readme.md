# [CHRONICLE NAME]: Changeling Character Creation Template

---

This template outlines the core steps for creating **Changeling: The Dreaming** characters in a [Mind’s Eye Theatre / OWBN / custom] chronicle. It can be adapted by local storytellers to reflect setting-specific rulings, approved splats, and house rules. Use this as a foundation for both Seelie, Unseelie, and Nunnehi (or other Kithain) concepts.

## Character Creation Steps

1. **Choose a Kith**  
   - [Insert list of approved Kiths in your chronicle: Sidhe, Satyr, Nocker, Troll, Sluagh, Redcap, Pooka, etc.]
   - For **Nunnehi**, Hsien, or **Gallain**, additional approvals may be required.

2. **Choose a Seeming**  
   - Seemings represent age and perception: **Childling**, **Wilder**, or **Grump**.
   - This will affect Banality thresholds, Glamour costs, and roleplaying expectations.

3. **Choose Court Affiliation**  
   - **Seelie** or **Unseelie**, based on your character’s driving ideals and interpretation of Dreaming philosophy.
   - Some chronicles may allow **Courtless**, **Shadow Court**, or local variants.

4. **Assign Attributes**  
   - Base: 7/5/3 split between **Physical**, **Social**, and **Mental** categories.
   - Additional traits may be bought at 1 point each.
   - [Specify any local attribute caps.]

5. **Assign Abilities**  
   - Distribute dots among Talents, Skills, and Knowledges as per standard rules.
   - [Specify any house restrictions on starting Ability levels.]

6. **Choose a Legacy**  
   - Choose **one Seelie** and **one Unseelie** Legacy.
   - Your active Legacy determines **how you regain Glamour**.

7. **Assign Glamour, Willpower, and Banality**  
   - Base stats are determined by Seeming and Kith.
   - Additional points may be bought using freebies or XP.
   - [Specify if Banality can be adjusted at creation.]

8. **Choose Arts & Realms**  
   - You begin with **3 dots of Arts** and **5 dots of Realms**.
   - Distribute as desired but remain within house-approved Arts (e.g., Chicanery, Primal, Soothsay).
   - [List banned or limited Arts/Realms if applicable.]

9. **Assign Backgrounds**  
   - Up to **5 different Backgrounds** at creation, maximum **3 dots each**.
   - [Specify available backgrounds: Holdings, Chimera, Title, Dreamers, etc.]

10. **Choose Merits and Flaws**  
   - Up to **7 points in Flaws** may be taken for bonus Freebie Points.
   - Merits must be approved by the ST.
   - Chronicle may limit or restrict certain supernatural Merits.

11. **Spend Freebie Points**  
   - You begin with **15 Freebie Points**.
   - Local STs may allow a higher or lower number.
   - Freebie costs:
     - Attribute: 5 / dot
     - Ability: 2 / dot
     - Background: 1 / dot
     - Glamour: 2 / dot
     - Willpower: 1 / dot
     - Banality: 1 / dot (to lower)
     - Arts: 5 / dot
     - Realms: 2 / dot
     - Merits/Flaws: 1:1

12. **Gain Starting XP**  
   - Default is **30 XP**, unless otherwise set by ST.
   - Use standard XP costs unless modified by house rules.

13. **Submit Sheet and Background**  
   - Character backgrounds must be submitted within **[X days]** of approval.
   - Background justifies points in Holdings, Title, Dreamers, Chimera, and other sensitive elements.

## Advancement & House Notes

- Character builds are capped at **160 creation points** (before XP/freebies), unless otherwise noted.
- No Traits or Abilities may exceed **5** at creation without ST approval.
- Advanced Arts are **not** available at character creation without R&U approval.
- Characters may not begin play with **multiple powerful Holdings**, **Titles above Baron**, or **Noble Status** without approval.

## Rare and Unusual (R&U) Characters

The following require R&U status:

- Sidhe of Autumn or pre-Shattering lineage
- Nunnehi, Hsien, Inanimae, Gallain
- Seeming/Glamour exceptions (e.g., dual Court, Nightfolk, Changeling/Vampire hybrids)
- Characters with holdings beyond standard

All R&U characters must include:

- Sheet
- Full background
- Legacy explanation
- Any custom mechanics or system proposals

## Sample: Changeling Character (Wilder Troll)

- Kith: Troll  
- Seeming: Wilder  
- Court: Seelie  
- Legacy: Noble Savage / Trickster  
- Attributes: Physical 7 / Social 5 / Mental 3  
- Abilities: Athletics 3, Leadership 2, Crafts 2, Kenning 1, Melee 3  
- Arts: Primal 2, Wayfare 1  
- Realms: Actor 1, Fae 1, Nature 1, Scene 1, Prop 1  
- Glamour: 4  
- Willpower: 3  
- Banality: 5  
- Backgrounds: Holdings 2, Chimera 1, Mentor 1  
- Merits: Huge Size (4)  
- Flaws: Chimerical Magnet (2), Code of Honor (2), Vow (1)  
- Freebies: 15 spent on Willpower, Glamour, Arts  
- Starting XP: 30  
- Notes: Player submitted backstory involving post-Stolen Prophecy interactions, noble lineage in exile.

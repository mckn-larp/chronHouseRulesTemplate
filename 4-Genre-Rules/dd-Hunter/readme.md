# [CHRONICLE NAME]: Hunter the Reckoning Character Creation Template

---

This template outlines the character creation process for **Hunter: The Reckoning** using the *Laws of the Hunt* LARP system. Storyteller approval is required for characters deviating from standard creation or involving crossover elements. Use this to design your Imbued hero, their Edges, and their purpose in the World of Darkness.

## Character Creation Steps

1. **Choose a Creed**  
   Select the guiding principle that drives your character’s hunt:
   - **Avenger** – Justice through punishment
   - **Defender** – Protection of the innocent
   - **Innocent** – Belief in redemption and hope
   - **Judge** – Seeks to understand the truth before acting
   - **Martyr** – Sacrifices self to protect others
   - **Redeemer** – Converts monsters or protects their humanity
   - **Visionary** – Seeks to understand the supernatural and its role

2. **Choose a Virtue**  
   Each Creed aligns with one of three Virtues:
   - **Mercy**, **Vision**, or **Zeal**

3. **Select Nature and Demeanor**  
   Reflects the internal motivation and outward behavior. Determines Willpower refresh through roleplay.

4. **Assign Attributes (Physical, Social, Mental)**  
   - Distribute **7/5/3** among the three categories.  
   - [Adjust if your chronicle uses different balance or caps.]

5. **Assign Abilities**  
   - Distribute **11 dots** across Talents, Skills, and Knowledges.  
   - No single Ability may exceed 3 dots at character creation without justification.

6. **Assign Backgrounds**  
   - Distribute **5 dots** among allowed Backgrounds.  
   - Common Hunter Backgrounds include: **Allies, Mentor, Contacts, Resources, Arsenal, Safehouse**.  
   - [Define banned or restricted Backgrounds.]

7. **Determine Traits**  
   - Start with **Willpower: 3**  
   - Start with **Conviction: 3**  
   - Spend **7 points** among **Virtue Traits**: Mercy, Vision, and Zeal.  
     - Must place at least **3 in your Creed's Virtue**.  
     - Max 5 in any Virtue at start without ST approval.

8. **Choose Edges**  
   - Select **3 Edges**:
     - One **Level One Edge** from any of your Creed’s associated Virtues.
     - One **Level Two Edge** from your Creed's primary Virtue.
     - One **Level Three Edge**, usually also from your primary Virtue.
   - All Edges must be **associated with your character’s Virtue Traits**.  
   - Some Edges may require **ST approval** or in-play development.  
   - [Define house-rulings on cross-Virtue Edge use.]

9. **Select Merits and Flaws**  
   - May take up to **7 points of Flaws** for an equal number of **Freebie Points**.  
   - Merits must be pre-approved.  
   - [List banned Merits/Flaws, such as True Faith or Vampire Ghouling.]

10. **Spend Freebie Points (15)**  
   - Attribute: 5 points/dot  
   - Ability: 2 points/dot  
   - Background: 1 point/dot  
   - Virtue Trait: 2 points/dot  
   - Willpower: 1 point/dot  
   - Conviction: 3 points/dot  
   - Edge: 7 points per level

11. **Starting XP (Optional)**  
   - Characters may receive **30 XP** at storyteller discretion.  
   - XP may be spent on traits per OWBN costs or chronicle standards.

12. **Create Your Origin**  
   - Chronicle-specific requirement: submit a short **origin story** of your Imbuing.  
   - Describe what awakened your character to the supernatural.  
   - Include who they were before and how the hunt changed them.

13. **Submit for Approval**  
   - All sheets must be submitted for review prior to play.  
   - Backgrounds, Edges, and Merits/Flaws should be justified.  
   - Chronicle staff may request edits or clarification.

## Optional Rules / House Adjustments

Include if your game uses any of the following:

### The Imbuing Event  
Describe how first-time characters are imbued in your setting.  
- Are mass Imbuings allowed?  
- Are Redeemers awakened only through trauma?

### Edge Availability  
List any banned Edges or those requiring in-play discovery.  
Examples:
- **Cleave**, **Witness**, **Suspend**, or high-level **Wayward** powers.

### Chronicle-Specific Paths  
Creeds may have localized philosophies:
- A Martyr driven by grief and loss
- A Defender trained in paramilitary protection
- A Visionary who interprets the Imbuing as prophecy

STs may ask for this to be reflected in Edges, Virtue splits, and Background use.

---

## Rare & Unusual Characters

Require pre-approval with background:

- **Waywards**
- **Imbued with other templates**
- **Demons or supernatural possession**
- **Hunters with access to True Faith, Numina, or magic**
- **Imbued Minors (under 18)**

## Sample: Redeemer Hunter

- **Creed**: Redeemer  
- **Virtue**: Mercy  
- **Nature/Demeanor**: Caregiver / Penitent  
- **Attributes**: Physical 3 / Social 7 / Mental 5  
- **Abilities**: Empathy 3, Streetwise 2, Occult 3, Subterfuge 1, Medicine 2  
- **Virtue Traits**: Mercy 4, Vision 2, Zeal 1  
- **Edges**:  
  - **Innocence (Level 1 Mercy)**  
  - **Reassure (Level 2 Mercy)**  
  - **Peace (Level 3 Mercy)**  
- **Backgrounds**: Contacts 2, Mentor 1, Safehouse 2  
- **Freebie Points**: Spent on Edge and Virtue boost  
- **XP**: Banked  
- **Imbuing Event**: She saw a vampire murder her partner and couldn’t look away — the voices called her to speak truth to the monster, and it fled.

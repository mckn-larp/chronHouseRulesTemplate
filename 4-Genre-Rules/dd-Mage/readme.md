# [CHRONICLE NAME]: Mage the Ascension Character Creation Template (Laws of Ascension)

---

This template outlines the step-by-step process for creating a **Tradition, Technocrat, or Disparate Mage** character in a LARP using the *Laws of Ascension* ruleset. Storyteller approval is required for non-standard concepts, crossover templates, or Rare & Unusual requests.

## Character Creation Steps

1. **Choose Affiliation & Faction**  
   Select one of the primary magical affiliations:
   - **Traditions** (e.g., Verbena, Dreamspeakers, Euthanatos)
   - **Technocratic Union** (e.g., Iteration X, NWO)
   - **Disparate Crafts** (e.g., Hollow Ones, Taftani)

2. **Choose Nature & Demeanor**  
   These describe your inner drive and outward behavior. Roleplaying your Nature can restore **Willpower**.

3. **Assign Attributes**  
   Distribute **7/5/3** among Physical, Social, and Mental categories.
   - [Adjust if your chronicle uses different Attribute caps.]

4. **Assign Abilities**  
   Distribute **13 points** among Talents, Skills, and Knowledges.
   - No single Ability should exceed **3 dots** at creation without strong justification.

5. **Assign Traits & Backgrounds**  
   - Begin with **Willpower: 3**
   - Backgrounds: Choose up to **5 dots**.
     - Common backgrounds include: Avatar, Node, Mentor, Resources, Sanctum, etc.
     - Backgrounds must be justified in your character's backstory.
     - [Specify any banned or restricted Backgrounds.]

6. **Assign Spheres of Magic**  
   - Choose **3 dots** of Spheres freely.
   - One Sphere must be designated as your **specialty Sphere**, representing your paradigm's focus.
   - No Sphere may start above **2** without ST approval.

7. **Assign Arete and Resonance**  
   - Default starting **Arete** is **2**
     - Arete 3 may be allowed with background and ST approval.
   - Choose **Resonance**: Dynamic, Entropic, or Static (1 dot total).
     - Optional: one descriptor per dot (e.g., “Fiery Dynamic Resonance”).

8. **Choose Paradigm, Practice, and Instruments**  
   - Define how your magic works:
     - **Paradigm**: Your worldview (e.g., “All is Mind,” “Technology as Enlightenment”).
     - **Practice**: Your method (e.g., Martial Arts, Hermetic Rituals, Psychotherapy).
     - **Instruments**: Tools and foci used to cast magic.

9. **Choose Merits & Flaws**  
   - May take up to **7 points of Flaws** for an equal number of bonus points.
   - Merits may require ST approval.
   - [List banned Merits/Flaws like True Faith or access to other supernatural templates.]

10. **Spend Freebie Points (15 Total)**  
   - Attribute: 5 points/dot  
   - Ability: 2 points/dot  
   - Background: 1 point/dot  
   - Sphere: 7 points/dot  
   - Arete: 4 points/dot  
   - Willpower: 1 point/dot  
   - Resonance: 2 points/dot  
   - Familiar: 6 points  
   - Node/Sanctum: Per Background rules

11. **Gain Starting XP (Optional)**  
   - Some chronicles award **30 XP** at start.  
   - Must be spent per standard XP costs from *Laws of Ascension*.

12. **Submit Background**  
   - Required to justify traits, backgrounds, and affiliations.
   - Must be submitted within **90 days** of first play.
   - Traits tied to unsent backgrounds may be removed at ST discretion.

## Optional & Chronicle-Specific Additions

### Magical Style Approval  
- [Note any restrictions on paradigms or practices. For example, ban “Coincidental-only Technocrats” or limit blood magic.]

### Rituals & Effects  
- Starting mages may submit **one Common Effect** at creation for automatic success.  
  - Must be tied to their Sphere(s) and practice.  
  - Effects should be thematic and non-abusive.

### Common Restricted Mechanics  
- **Arete 3+ at start** requires backstory & ST approval.  
- **Sphere 3+** not allowed at creation.  
- **Cross-sphere effects** require ST oversight.  
- **Time effects** that alter scene timing may be restricted.

### Resonance Examples  
- **Dynamic:** “Chaotic,” “Flaming,” “Unstable”
- **Static:** “Mechanical,” “Sterile,” “Geometric”
- **Entropic:** “Decaying,” “Whispering,” “Rotten”

---

## Rare & Unusual Concepts

The following must be approved via ST request and background:

- **Marauders**
- **Nephandi**
- **Orphan Traditions or custom Crafts**
- **True Faith mages**
- **Crossover templates** (e.g., vampire-blooded mages)
- **Avatar level 5**

## Sample Mage Character

- **Faction**: Tradition (Verbena)  
- **Nature/Demeanor**: Survivor / Rebel  
- **Attributes**: Physical 3 / Social 5 / Mental 7  
- **Abilities**: Rituals 3, Survival 2, Medicine 3, Esoterica 2, Intimidation 2, Occult 1  
- **Spheres**: Life 2 (specialty), Spirit 1  
- **Arete**: 2  
- **Willpower**: 4  
- **Resonance**: Entropic (Rotten)  
- **Paradigm**: “All is Life and Decay”  
- **Practice**: Blood rites and herbalism  
- **Instruments**: Carved bone athame, iron cauldron, wolf’s fang pendant  
- **Backgrounds**: Avatar 2, Node 1, Mentor 2  
- **Merits/Flaws**: Danger Sense (1), Echoes (2), Haunted (3)
